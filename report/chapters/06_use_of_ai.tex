This project used AI assistants, specifically Claude Code and ChatGPT, as
productivity tools during development and writing. They assisted with routine
code generation and refactoring, code review, bug-location during the audit,
prose drafts revised by the authors, and diagram rendering from author-specified
layouts.

The intellectual core is the authors' own: system architecture, engine
selection, benchmark design, research questions, and interpretation. Every
benchmark figure reported here was produced by our own runs, and every external
reference was verified by the authors before citation.

The authors take full responsibility for the correctness, originality, and
verification of all code, data, and conclusions in this report. Where AI tools
contributed text or code, that output was read, checked, and where necessary
corrected and rewritten by the authors, who treat it as a draft to be
scrutinised rather than as an authority to be trusted.
